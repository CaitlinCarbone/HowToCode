About The Website

Our website will provide a comprehensive platform for individuals who are interested in learning about coding and want to become proficient in managing code and version control. This website will offer a variety of resources, including information pages on different types of coding languages, all of which will allow for the user to explore and understand the specifics of each language. Furthermore, it’ll provide resources on how to use GitHub commands, ensuring that users will be able to effectively navigate this tool. Additionally, our site will offer informative pages on popular digital platforms such as Discord and Facebook, further improving the user’s digital literacy of these services. Lastly, users will be able to have some hands on experience with our interactive coding snippets.

Purpose

In the modern age, coding has become essential in many professions, requiring workers to understand the fundamentals. However, not all office workers have the luxury of time to take extensive online courses and majority are unlikely to remember everything they learn. Our website strives to address this issue by serving as a widely accessible and easily comprehensible platform.
